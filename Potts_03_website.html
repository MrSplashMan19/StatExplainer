- Stat Explainer - 

Ryan "Splash" Potts

Stats are seemingly created out of the blue, so new sports fans are always chasing the learning curve. Here at statexplainer.com, we have all the tools for the most casual fan to the most hardcore fan. No stat is too big or too small to be included here. If you ever want a new stat added (or a new sport), send an email to Ryan.       

Ryan has been a sports fan and statistician since 2006. In 2019, he began writing and creating audio sports content. He currently writes for Prime Time Sports Talk and Overtime Heroics. He is a cohost of the Cheap Seats Chatter podcast on the Overtime Heroics network, and he is the host of The Sports Desk, a live show hosted on the uSTADIUM Live Network. Ryan has worked for uSTADIUM since July 2019, and he has been the lead producer since the introduction of the live network in January 2021.             

Basic Baseball

Batting Average, ERA, Errors, and More! 

Get your sea legs here by learning the ins and outs of baseball's stats. After reading this, you'll be able to amaze your friends the next time you enjoy America's pastime. 

Advanced Baseball

wRC+, SIERA, DRS, and More!

So you've watched Moneyball? You've come to the right place to dig deeper into baseball's biggest stats. The counting stats are fun, but rate stats separate the men from the boys.    

Basic Football

Passer Rating, Sacks, Kickoffs, and More! 

They say numbers never lie. Here, you can determine the legitimacy of that statement by looking at what various stats mean. Some stats are more useful than others.

Advanced Football

ANY/A+, Elusive Rating, Pressures, and More!

Do you want to step up your NFL or NCAA football game? Here we go beyond the game into the theoretical realm. Dust off your VHS tapes, it is time for a film study.

Basic Basketball

Turnovers, Triple-Doubles, Rebounds, and More!

Let's dig into the boxscore to find the best players. How can you figure out who is playing well? While not perfect, these stats give you a leg up on comparing Michael Jordan to DeAndre Jordan. 

Advanced Basketball

PER, Win Shares, VORP, and More!

It looks like alphabet soup, but basketball's advanced stats will be tasty after you read through what each one means. You'll probably like PIE a lot. :)   

Basic Hockey

Points, Save Percentage, Penalty Minutes, and More!

Head to the faceoff circle and learn some simple hockey stats. Keep your head on a swivel and discover the world where fighting is only punished with a five-minute penalty.   

Advanced Hockey

Plus-Minus, GAA, Point Share, and More! 

Hoist Lord Stanley's cup with these fancy numbers. You know that Wayne Greztky is good, but just how good was "The Great One." You can find out here!
